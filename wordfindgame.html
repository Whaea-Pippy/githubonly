How to Play AI VIBE

Connect letters to form key AI concepts hidden in the grid. Words can snake horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Find all the main concepts for each level to progress and learn about AI, prompting, and creative coding.

Scoring

+1 point per letter in main list concepts.

+5 points per letter in bonus concepts (from any level).

Stars

Click a star when stuck to refresh the board (15 total).

Learning Log

Tracks every concept you find, time taken, and points. Copy it at the end for reflection or sharing.

Tips

Bonus concepts hide among filler letters. Later levels get more challenging with advanced ideas.

Experiment with prompts in real life – change one word and see how the "vibe" shifts!

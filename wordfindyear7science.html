How to Play SCI VIBE

Connect letters to form key science words hidden in the grid. Words can snake horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Find all 5 words in each level to move up and learn Year 7 & 8 science vocabulary.

Scoring

+1 point per letter in main list words.

+5 points per letter in bonus words (from any level).

Stars

Click a star when stuck to refresh the board (15 total).

Learning Log

Tracks every word you find, time taken, and points. Click “Copy” at the end, then paste into your Google Site – columns line up perfectly!

Tips

Bonus words hide among filler letters. Later levels get trickier with longer words.

Look for patterns – science words love to connect!
